Application Concept


A web tool that allows you to find the best gear in town.

Quickly sell things you list and find great deals nearby.

An amazingly simple and different way to buy and sell.

Users login/create a profile key with their email.

UID tracks session and logs all activity to Firebase under UID.

Fast and Innovative

Post an item for sale in just a minute.

Easy, Sleek and Intuitive.

Show you great deals nearby on a huge variety of items.

Browse your preferred area or search for something specific.

Get ahold with other buyers and sellers.

Simple to use interface.

Motivation for Development


We got some of our inspiration from KSL Classified and wanted to expand to include other states.

Facebook also played a role in coming up with this idea, there are so many yard sale pages to look through.

We wanted an all in one place where we could find the local garage sales, yard sales and/or estate sales.
